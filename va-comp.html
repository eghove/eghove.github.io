VA Disability Compensation Estimator

eterans who incurred a medical condition as the result of their military service may qualify for monthly disability compensation benefits administered by the U.S. Department of Veterans Affairs (the VA). These rates are determined by several factors including the combined evaluation for compensation and can be difficult to find on VA.gov, the VA's website. The VA Disability Compensation Estimator allows the user to answer a few questions and dynamically displays changes to the monthly disability compensation rate.
